Vision Statement

FAWEREH has two centers of interest, namely;

  • (a) Issues of reproductive health and
  • (b) Human exploitation by traffickers and scammers.

  • -We offer an affordable solution to reproductive health care.
  • -We fight against the social suffering of infertile couples and gender preference
  • -We promote sex education in schools and homes
  • -We enable people to have good lives in their home countries by promotion specialization and entrepreneurship.

Goals

Goals 1
  • Fight against human trafficking by means of sensitization and fostering family well being
  • Orientate people about the dangers or damages they may encounter abroad as a result of misinformation by traffickers
  • Create job opportunities
Goals 2
  • Fight against social suffering of infertility
  • Fight against the causes of infertility
  • Educate the population on reproductive health and sex education
  • Apply measures to reduce infant and maternal mortality rate
  • Apply measures in reducing the high rate of infertility in Cameroon and Africa in general.
  • Fight against HIV/AIDs and STIs
  • Address issues on gender preference
